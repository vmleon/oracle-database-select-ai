NEW EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING CHECKLIST
Acme Corporation

BEFORE YOUR FIRST DAY

Your manager and HR will coordinate the following before you arrive:
- Laptop and equipment ordered and configured
- Email account and system access provisioned
- Building access badge prepared
- Desk or workspace assigned
- Welcome kit assembled (company swag, office supplies, org chart)

DAY 1 - WELCOME AND ORIENTATION

Morning:
- Check in at the front desk; collect your badge and welcome kit
- Meet your manager for a welcome coffee
- Tour of the office: restrooms, kitchen, meeting rooms, emergency exits
- IT setup session: laptop configuration, email, Slack, VPN, and password setup
- Review and sign employment documents with HR

Afternoon:
- Company overview presentation by HR (mission, values, org structure)
- Benefits enrollment walkthrough
- Set up direct deposit and tax forms
- Meet your onboarding buddy (assigned peer mentor for your first 90 days)
- Lunch with your immediate team

WEEK 1 - GETTING STARTED

Required training (complete by end of week 1):
- Information Security Awareness training (online, 45 minutes)
- Anti-Harassment and Inclusion training (online, 60 minutes)
- Data Privacy and GDPR compliance (online, 30 minutes)
- Code of Conduct acknowledgment
- Emergency procedures and safety training (15 minutes)

Key meetings:
- 1:1 with your manager to discuss role expectations, 30-60-90 day goals
- Team standup or weekly meeting
- Introduction meetings with key cross-functional partners
- Schedule recurring 1:1s with your manager (weekly recommended)

Systems and tools to set up:
- Slack: join your team channel and #general, #random, #announcements
- HR portal: review your profile, emergency contacts, benefits selections
- Concur: set up your travel and expense profile
- JIRA/project management tool: get access to relevant boards
- GitHub/source control: set up SSH keys and repository access

WEEKS 2-4 - RAMPING UP

- Complete any role-specific technical training
- Shadow team members on key workflows and processes
- Begin contributing to projects with guidance from your buddy or manager
- Attend department all-hands meeting
- Complete your benefits enrollment (deadline: 30 days from start date)
- Review company intranet and knowledge base resources

DAYS 30-60 - BUILDING MOMENTUM

- 30-day check-in with manager: review initial goals, address questions
- Take ownership of at least one small project or task
- Provide feedback on the onboarding experience to HR
- Connect with colleagues outside your immediate team
- Begin attending optional brown bag lunches and learning sessions

DAYS 60-90 - FULL INTEGRATION

- 60-day check-in with manager: assess progress, adjust goals if needed
- Contribute independently to team projects
- 90-day formal review with manager: performance discussion and goal setting for the next quarter
- Onboarding buddy relationship formally concludes (informal mentoring may continue)
- Complete the onboarding satisfaction survey
