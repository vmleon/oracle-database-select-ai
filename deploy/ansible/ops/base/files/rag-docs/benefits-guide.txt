EMPLOYEE BENEFITS GUIDE
Acme Corporation

HEALTH INSURANCE

Acme Corporation offers three health insurance plans through BlueCross BlueShield:

1. Basic Plan: $75/month employee-only, $200/month family
   - $1,500 individual deductible, $3,000 family deductible
   - 80/20 coinsurance after deductible
   - $5,000 out-of-pocket maximum

2. Standard Plan: $150/month employee-only, $400/month family
   - $750 individual deductible, $1,500 family deductible
   - 90/10 coinsurance after deductible
   - $3,000 out-of-pocket maximum

3. Premium Plan: $250/month employee-only, $600/month family
   - $250 individual deductible, $500 family deductible
   - 100% covered after deductible
   - $1,000 out-of-pocket maximum

All plans include preventive care at no cost, prescription drug coverage, and mental health services. Acme Corporation covers 70% of premium costs for all plans.

DENTAL INSURANCE

Dental coverage is provided through Delta Dental at $25/month for individual coverage and $65/month for family coverage. Coverage includes two preventive cleanings per year at no cost, basic procedures at 80% coverage, and major procedures at 50% coverage. Annual maximum benefit is $2,000 per person.

VISION INSURANCE

Vision coverage is provided through VSP at $10/month for individual and $25/month for family. Benefits include an annual eye exam with a $10 copay, $150 allowance for frames every 24 months, and contact lens coverage up to $130 per year.

401(K) RETIREMENT PLAN

Acme Corporation offers a 401(k) retirement plan through Fidelity Investments. Key features:
- Employee contributions up to IRS annual limits
- Company match: 100% of the first 4% of salary contributed, plus 50% of the next 2%
- Maximum employer match: 5% of salary
- Immediate vesting on employee contributions
- Employer match vests over 3 years: 33% after year 1, 66% after year 2, 100% after year 3
- Both traditional (pre-tax) and Roth (post-tax) options available

LIFE INSURANCE

Acme Corporation provides basic life insurance at 2x annual salary at no cost to the employee, up to a maximum of $500,000. Supplemental life insurance is available for purchase at group rates up to 5x salary.

DISABILITY INSURANCE

Short-term disability: 60% of base salary for up to 26 weeks, with a 7-day waiting period. Provided at no cost.
Long-term disability: 60% of base salary after 26 weeks of disability, continuing until age 65. Employee cost is $15/month.

WELLNESS PROGRAMS

Acme Corporation offers a comprehensive wellness program including:
- $500 annual wellness reimbursement for gym memberships, fitness classes, or wellness apps
- Free annual health screenings and flu shots
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP) with 8 free counseling sessions per year
- Monthly wellness challenges with prizes
- Ergonomic workstation assessments

EDUCATION BENEFITS

Tuition reimbursement up to $5,250 per year for job-related courses and degree programs. Employees must maintain a grade of B or above and remain employed for 12 months after reimbursement, or repay the benefit.

COMMUTER BENEFITS

Pre-tax commuter benefits up to $300/month for transit passes and $300/month for qualified parking expenses.

ENROLLMENT

Benefits enrollment occurs annually during Open Enrollment (November 1-15). New hires have 30 days from their start date to enroll. Life events (marriage, birth, adoption) allow a 30-day special enrollment period.
