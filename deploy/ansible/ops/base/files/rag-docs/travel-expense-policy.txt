TRAVEL AND EXPENSE POLICY
Acme Corporation

GENERAL GUIDELINES

All business travel must be pre-approved by your manager. International travel requires VP-level approval. Book travel at least 14 days in advance when possible to secure the best rates. All bookings should be made through the company's approved travel platform (Concur).

AIR TRAVEL

- Domestic flights: Economy class for all employees
- International flights under 6 hours: Economy class
- International flights over 6 hours: Premium economy for all employees; business class for VP and above
- Book the lowest logical fare within a 2-hour window of your preferred departure time
- Frequent flyer miles earned on business travel belong to the employee

HOTEL ACCOMMODATIONS

Per-night hotel limits by city tier:
- Tier 1 cities (New York, San Francisco, London, Tokyo): $250/night
- Tier 2 cities (Chicago, Boston, Seattle, Los Angeles): $200/night
- Tier 3 cities (all others): $150/night

Exceptions may be approved by your manager for conferences where the event hotel exceeds these limits.

GROUND TRANSPORTATION

- Use ride-sharing services (Uber, Lyft) or taxis for airport transfers and local transportation
- Rental cars are approved when ride-sharing is impractical or when traveling between multiple locations
- Rental cars must be mid-size or below; SUVs allowed only with justification
- Mileage reimbursement for personal vehicle use: $0.67 per mile (IRS standard rate)
- Parking and tolls are reimbursable with receipts

MEALS AND PER DIEM

Daily meal allowances (including tax and tip):
- Breakfast: $20
- Lunch: $30
- Dinner: $50
- Total daily maximum: $100

Alcohol is not reimbursable except for client entertainment with prior manager approval. Team dinners during travel are reimbursable up to $75 per person with manager approval.

CLIENT ENTERTAINMENT

Client meals and entertainment require pre-approval and must include:
- Business purpose documented on the expense report
- Names and titles of all attendees
- Company/organization of external attendees
- Maximum $150 per person for client dinners

EXPENSE REPORTING

- Submit expense reports within 14 days of travel completion
- Receipts required for all expenses over $25
- Use the Concur expense system for all submissions
- Reports are routed to your manager for approval
- Reimbursement is processed within 5 business days of approval via direct deposit

NON-REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES

The following are not reimbursable:
- Personal entertainment (movies, spa, mini-bar)
- Laundry service for trips under 5 days
- First-class upgrades (unless pre-approved)
- Spouse/partner travel expenses
- Traffic violations or parking tickets
- Personal phone calls exceeding $10/day
- Travel insurance (company policy provides coverage)

CORPORATE CREDIT CARD

Employees who travel frequently (4+ trips per year) may request a corporate credit card. The card must be used exclusively for business expenses. Personal charges on the corporate card are a policy violation and may result in card revocation and disciplinary action.
