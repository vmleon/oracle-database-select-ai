EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK
Acme Corporation

COMPANY VALUES

At Acme Corporation, we are guided by four core values: Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, and Customer Focus. Every employee is expected to embody these values in their daily work and interactions with colleagues, partners, and customers.

CODE OF CONDUCT

All employees must maintain professional behavior in the workplace. This includes treating colleagues with respect and dignity, avoiding conflicts of interest, and protecting company confidential information. Harassment, discrimination, or retaliation of any kind will not be tolerated and may result in immediate termination.

Employees must not use company resources for personal gain or engage in activities that could damage the company's reputation. Any gifts from vendors or partners exceeding $50 in value must be reported to your manager and the Ethics team.

REMOTE WORK POLICY

Acme Corporation supports flexible work arrangements. Employees in eligible roles may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Remote workers must maintain a dedicated workspace, be available during core business hours (10:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time), and attend all required in-person meetings.

Fully remote positions are available for certain roles and require VP-level approval. Remote employees are expected to visit the office at least once per quarter for team-building events.

DRESS CODE

Business casual attire is expected Monday through Thursday. Fridays are casual dress days. When meeting with clients or attending formal events, business professional attire is required.

CONFIDENTIALITY

All employees sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement upon hiring. Company trade secrets, customer data, financial information, and strategic plans must be kept strictly confidential. Violations may result in termination and legal action.

WORKPLACE SAFETY

Acme Corporation is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment. Report any safety hazards, accidents, or near-misses to your manager and the Safety team immediately. All employees must complete annual safety training.

COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES

Official company communications go through designated channels. Employees should use company email for business correspondence and Slack for internal collaboration. Social media posts about the company must be approved by the Communications team.
